M-Pesa is the backbone of the Kenyan economy, but its convenience often leads to "shrapnel spending"â€”those small daily transactions that add up to significant amounts by the end of the month.

In this guide, we'll show you how to take control of your M-Pesa statement and turn it into a powerful financial growth tool.

## 1. Categorize Your Transactions

Most M-Pesa users only look at their balance. To really track your money, you need to know where it's going. Divide your spending into:

- **Essential Bills** (Rent, Tokens, Water)
- **Daily Living** (Groceries, Fare)
- **Business/Work** (Supplies, Data)
- **Lifestyle** (Dining, Entertainment)

## 2. Separate Personal and Business

If you're an entrepreneur, never pay personal bills from your Till or Paybill. It makes bookkeeping a nightmare.
